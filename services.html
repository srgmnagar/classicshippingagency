Compliance & Brokerage

Custom House Agency (CHA)

Operating as a fully licensed Customs Broker (License: CHA-1998-IND) for nearly three decades, we manage complex import and export regulatory protocols at India's major marine terminals. We clear your cargo swiftly while ensuring full adherence to tariff classifications, valuation audits, duty assessments, and government trade guidelines.

Automated Customs Interface

Direct EDI connection via ICEGATE for immediate cargo declaration, entry filings, and duty processing.

Classification & Exemption Auditing

Expert tariff consulting ensuring your goods benefit from eligible bilateral trade agreements and duty exemptions.

Port & Custom House Representation

Our experienced dock operations officers handle physical inspections, clearing cargo under customs supervision.

Vessel Agency Services

Complete Ship Husbandry
