About Our Agency

Home About Us
Established 1998

Steering Indian Maritime Logistics for Over 28 Years

Classic Shipping Agency was founded in 1998 with a single mission: to simplify the complex world of customs clearance and vessel coordination for international merchants docking in Gujarat.

As Bhavnagar and Pipavav ports expanded into major national trade corridors, we grew alongside them. By securing early Custom House Agent (CHA) operations and building robust local networks, we became a dependable partner for vessel owners and charterers. Today, we handle millions of tons of dry bulk, containerized shipments, and liquid cargo, maintaining the same integrity and focus on regulatory compliance that defined our first voyage in 1998.

Key Milestones in Our Journey

1998

Foundation of Classic Shipping Agency

Established in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, initiating our maritime, vessel agency, logistics, and shipping support operations. Built on a foundation of industry expertise, deep commitment to reliable maritime services, and fostering long-term customer relationships.

2005

Pipavav Port Branch Expansion

Opened our dedicated branch office at Pipavav Port, Gujarat (India's first private port) to manage specialized vessel husbandry and containerized cargo booking.

2014

Fleet & Multi-Modal Logistics

Expanded inland capabilities by securing partner warehousing and operating a dedicated fleet of container flatbeds, linking sea routes directly to rail and road.

2026

Leading Enterprise Network

Coordinating shipping agency, air freight, customs brokerage, and cargo bookings across major ports nationwide, serving over 5000+ clients and managing thousands of vessel voyages.
