A Legacy of Logistics Excellence and Maritime Credibility

Founded in 1998 in Gujarat, Classic Shipping Agency has grown from a regional custom clearance agency into a nationwide multi-modal logistics partner. We provide vessel owners, charterers, and international merchants with a single point of execution for port logistics, container management, and regulatory compliance.
